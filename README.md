# DigiFarmLink -- Farmer-to-Buyer Digital Marketplace Platform

## Overview

DigiFarmLink is a digital marketplace that connects vegetable farmers
directly with buyers while coordinating transport providers to reduce
post-harvest losses, improve pricing transparency, and simplify
agricultural logistics in Ghana.

## Business Problem

-   Limited market access for farmers
-   Dependence on middlemen
-   High post-harvest losses
-   Poor transport coordination
-   Limited market information
-   Difficulty for buyers to find trusted suppliers
-   Lack of transparent pricing

## Objectives

-   Connect farmers directly with buyers
-   Reduce post-harvest losses
-   Improve price transparency
-   Increase farmer income
-   Coordinate logistics
-   Reduce delivery time
-   Build buyer trust
-   Support low-bandwidth environments
-   Create a scalable marketplace

## Business Rules

-   Farmers sell only their own produce.
-   Buyers must register before ordering.
-   Transport begins after order confirmation.
-   Only verified transporters accept deliveries.
-   Listings expire automatically.
-   Reviews only after completed orders.
